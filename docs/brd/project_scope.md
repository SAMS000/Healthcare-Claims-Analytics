# Project Scope

## Project Title

Healthcare Claims Analytics & Business Analysis Platform

## Objective

Develop an end-to-end healthcare analytics platform that extracts, transforms, stores, and analyzes publicly available Medicare provider payment data.

The project simulates a real-world healthcare analytics workflow by combining ETL pipelines, SQL analysis, Excel reporting, and Business Analyst documentation.

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of provider payment data.

Business analysts need reliable processes to:

- Analyze provider utilization
- Monitor Medicare payments
- Identify payment trends
- Produce business reports
- Support operational decision making

## Goals

- Build ETL pipelines
- Design a relational database
- Perform SQL analytics
- Generate Excel reports
- Produce Business Analyst artifacts
